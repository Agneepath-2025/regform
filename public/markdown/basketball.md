#   **Basketball**  
*Men and Women*

**Format:**

1. Minimum Squad Size 7; Maximum Squad Size 12

2. All matches will be 5v5; full court, with substitutions only allowed on a dead ball. 

**Rules:**

1. Rules will stay the same throughout quarterfinals, semifinals and finals.

2. The team that scores more **points** by the end wins. 

3. Time Limit: **40 mins** (the team that has scored more number of points progresses to the next round).

   1. Quarter time: **10 mins**

   2. **2 Mins** break between each quarter with half time for **5 Mins**

   3. 3 breaks, 1 after every 10 minutes

      

4. Timeout permitted for each team: 

   1. **First** half will have **2 time outs** 

   2. **Second** half will have **3 time outs**

   3. Each timeout will be **1 minute** long

5. Foul Outs: Same as the FIBA rulebook.    
6. Any participant committing more than 4 fouls will be asked to switch out for the rest of the match.  
7. A team will be in a team foul penalty situation after it has committed 4 team fouls in 1 quarter.

   *\*All Basic Basketball rules will apply in accordance with FIBA 2024 Guidelines \** [https://assets.fiba.basketball/image/upload/documents-corporate-fiba-official-rules-2024-v10a.pdf](https://assets.fiba.basketball/image/upload/documents-corporate-fiba-official-rules-2024-v10a.pdf)
