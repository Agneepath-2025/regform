# **Squash**

*Men and Women*

**Format:**

1. Format will either be a single elimination knockout bracket or group-round robins, and then knockouts (depending on the number of registered teams). (If a team fails to arrive within 15 minutes of their scheduled match time, a walkover will be awarded to the opponent.)

2. Each team shall consist a minimum of 3 maximum of 5 players (3 playing and 2 substitutes). The 3 participants will be divided in a random seed order. 

3. Each teamâ€™s match will be divided into 3 singles matches. Each singles match will be a **best of 5** games. 

**Rules:**

1. The 5 member team is separate for the men's tournament and women's tournament. 

2. All standard [WSF (SRFI) Rules](https://www.worldsquash.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/240102_Rules-of-Singles-Squash-2024-V1.2.pdf) will be followed for all matches.
