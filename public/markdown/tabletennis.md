# **Table Tennis** 

*Men & Women*

**Format:**

1.  2 Singles, Doubles and 2 Reverse Singles  
2. Each team (men’s and women’s) will consist of minimum 4 and maximum 5 participants.

**Game:** 

1. A game shall be won by the participant or pair first scoring 11 points unless both participants or pairs score 10 points, in which case the game shall be won by the first participant subsequently gaining a lead of 2 points.

**Match:** 

1. A match will consist of the best of 3 games and best of 5 for the finals.

**Clothing of the participants:** 

1. The main colour of a shirt, skirt or shorts, other than sleeves and collar of a shirt shall be clearly different from that of the ball in use, i.e., since a white ball will be used, participants must avoid wearing white clothing. 

**Order of play:** 

1. In singles, the server shall first make a service, the receiver shall then make a return and thereafter server and receiver alternately shall each make a return.

**Let:** 

1. A let is a rally of which the result is not scored. The rally shall be a let:  
   1. if in service the ball touches the net assembly, provided the service is otherwise correct or the ball is obstructed by the receiver   
   2. if the service is delivered when the receiving participant is not ready, provided that neither the receiver attempts to strike the ball  
   3. if failure to make a service or a return or otherwise to comply with the Laws is due to a disturbance outside the control of the participant  
   4. if the ball brushes the side of the table, anywhere outside the white line. On and inside the white line is acceptable
