# **Tennis**

*Mixed Teams*

**General Rules:**

1. Colleges can register at most one team, consisting of both men and women participants.  
2. Each team should register a minimum of 5 participants (3 men and 2 women) and a maximum of 9 participants (5 men and 4 women).  
3. The participants should bring their own equipment (except balls). 

**Game Rules:**  
Event: Mixed Team Tennis

1. Format of the rubber: Each rubber will consist of at most 5 matches \- Women's Singles, Men's Singles, Mixed Doubles, Men's Doubles and Women's Doubles. The team to win three matches out of these five matches wins the rubber.   
2. Format will either be 1 set or best of 9 games.  
3. ‘Deuce’ will be two points (i.e., with advantage).  
4. ‘Let’ will be a re-serve.  
5. Umpires will make the calls.   
6. Only 1 male participant and 2 female participants can play at most 2 matches in 1 rubber. No other participant can play more than 1 match in 1 rubber.   
7. Examples:   
   Let a team have A, B, C, D as their male participants and X, Y, Z as their female participants.   
   The following is a non-exhaustive list of possible combinations.  
     
     
     
     
   

| Men’s Singles | Women’s Singles | Mixed Doubles | Men’s Doubles | Women’s Doubles | Combination |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| A | X | C, X | B, D | Y, Z | Accepted |
| A | X | B, Y | A, C | X, Y | Accepted |
| A | X | D, Y | A, D | X, Y | Denied (Because two male participants are repeating) |
| B | X | D, X | A, D | X, Y | Denied (Because a female participant is playing thrice) |

All other standard tennis rules will apply throughout the tournament.
