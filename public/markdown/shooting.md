# **Shooting**

*Mixed Teams* 

**Categories**   
10 Meter Air Rifle (Mixed) Individual  
10 Meter Air Pistol (Mixed) Individual

### **Match Format**

1. Total Shots & Timing: 30 shots within 40 minutes.\*\*  
2. Practice & Sightline Time: 10 minutes before the match.  
3. Equipment:  
   1. Targets: Provided by the host university.  
   2. Other equipment (e.g., weapons, pellets, stands): To be arranged by participants.  
   3. All the equipment that the participants will be carrying will be deposited at the shooting range upon entry into campus.   
4. Rules: Any disputes will be resolved by the Range Officer, whose decision is final.

\*Finals will be in COC (10 shots) format between the top 5 participants in both rifle and pistol category. However, it is subject to change based on the total number of participants.   
\*\* Subject to total number of participants. 

**Winner Declaration**:   
Top 2 scorers in each category will be awarded position-holder titles.
